Kiwanis Park at Woodlawn

The Kiwanis Club of Stuart has been serving the children of Martin County since 1938. We are generous with our time, creative with our ideas, and passionate about making a difference in our community.

Explore Our Programs Join Our Club

Making a Difference, One Child at a Time

We bring together leaders who dedicate their time and talent to make a lasting impact in their community. From the first public pool to soccer fields, and now the award-winning Kiwanis Children's Park at Woodlawn, we're dedicated to improving the lives of children every single day.

Whether raising funds or volunteering, providing our community with much-needed assistance, we work with dedication and purpose. Our members are passionate, creative, and committed to making Martin County a better place for all children.
